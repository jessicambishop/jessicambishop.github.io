Hello, I’m Jessica!

I am a designer and writer focused on how learnability, storytelling, sensemaking, wayfinding, and removing barriers to learning can improve the learner’s experience.

“The purpose of a storyteller is not to tell you how to think, but to give you questions to think upon.” — Brandon Sanderson

Education and Experience

I am a graduate student in the Learning Technologies and Design (LTD) program and User Experience and Usability (UXU) graduate certificate at the University of Missouri. I plan to complete the program and certificate in Spring 2024. In 2005, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies (Honors) from Rochester University. Additionally, I have completed a variety of design and writing certificates from organizations such as: Interaction Design Foundation, Cengage Learning, Spring Arbor University, Indiana University, Open2Study, and LinkedIn Learning.

My prior experiences include an eclectic mix of retail, public libraries, technology, publishing, communications, and small business.
