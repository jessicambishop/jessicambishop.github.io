About Me

Hello, I’m Jessica!

I am a designer and writer focused on how learnability, storytelling, sensemaking, wayfinding, and removing barriers to learning can improve the learner’s experience.

Experience

I work on an eLearning team at a private university in Michigan. My prior experiences include an eclectic mix of retail, public libraries, technology, publishing, communications, and small business.

Education

I am a graduate student in the Learning Technologies and Design (LTD) program and User Experience and Usability (UXU) graduate certificate at the University of Missouri. I plan to complete the program and certificate in Spring 2024. In 2005, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies (Honors) from Rochester University.

Additionally, I have completed a variety of design and writing certificates from organizations such as:

  • Interaction Design Foundation
  • Cengage Learning
  • Spring Arbor University
  • Indiana University
  • Open2Study
  • LinkedIn Learning

Personal

I live in Michigan with my husband, our three-year-old son, and two cats. I enjoy reading, writing, crafting, walking, and spending way too much time in the distant corners of the internet.
